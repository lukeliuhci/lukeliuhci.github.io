Hi! I am a PhD student and HCI Researcher at the human-centered systems lab (h-lab) of Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (KIT).

My research lies at the intersection of **Human-Computer Interaction (HCI)** and **Human-Centered AI**. I am particularly interested in designing and evaluating **Extended Reality (XR)** environments leveraging eye-tracking and other biosignals, aiming to create hyper-personalized user experience. Recently, my work has focused on developing educational technologies that leverage GenAI and gaze-adaptive systems to support immersive learning.

Previously, I received my M.Sc. in Human-Computer Interaction from the University of Siegen and my B.Eng. in Industrial Design from the Beijing Institute of Technology. I have also collaborated on applied research and design with industry partners.

## Research Interests
* **Human-Computer Interaction (HCI)**
* **Human-Centered AI** & GenAI Integration in XR
* **Extended Reality (XR)** (VR/AR/MR)
* **Biosignal-adaptive Systems** (e.g., Eye-tracking and gaze-adaptive systems)
* **Educational Technologies** & Immersive Learning
